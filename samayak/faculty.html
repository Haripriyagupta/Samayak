Faculty Powers

What Faculty Can Do

Upload Attendance

Upload an Excel sheet with Enrollment Number, Student Name, and Attendance Percentage. System auto-populates every student's view.

Upload Marks

MST and internal marks uploaded per subject via Excel. Each student sees only their own subject-wise breakdown.

Manage Timetables

Upload regular, MST, practical, special event, or revised timetables. All versions are stored and accessible.

Post Announcements

Publish notices directly — assignment deadlines, exam dates, holiday announcements, or classroom changes.

Upload Syllabus

Share subject syllabi as PDF or document files. Students can view and download anytime.

Track Activity

All student signup and login events are automatically logged with timestamp for accountability.
