The Platform

How Samayak Works

Samayak is a role-based web portal with two distinct user types — Faculty and Students — each with carefully scoped permissions to protect privacy and ensure data integrity.

Faculty members upload bulk data — attendance, MST marks, internal marks, timetables, and announcements — directly via Excel sheets. The system automatically parses and maps records by enrollment number, updating every student's dashboard in real time.

Students log in to view only their own records. No student can access another's attendance or marks — privacy is enforced at the data layer, not just the UI.
