Other Key Features

Attendance Tracking

Subject-wise attendance updated via Excel. Students see their own percentage with a status indicator.

Marks & Results

MST and internal marks uploaded by subject. Full performance summary visible to each student.

Timetable

Regular, MST, practical, and special timetables uploaded and available to all students instantly.

Notices & Announcements

Faculty post notices directly on the portal — deadlines, exam notifications, or classroom updates.

Syllabus Access

Faculty upload PDFs or documents for each subject. Students download directly from their dashboard.

Role-Based Security

Faculty Verification Code + enrollment-number validation ensures only authorized users can register.
